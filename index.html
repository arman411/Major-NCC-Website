Who We Are

The NCC Unit of
Govt. Polytechnic Hamirpur (HP)

Building character, courage, and commitment in the youth of Himachal Pradesh since over two decades.

Patriotism & Discipline

We instil national pride and a disciplined lifestyle through structured training programs and ceremonial activities.

Community Service
