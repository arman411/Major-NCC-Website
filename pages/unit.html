Divisions

Our Wings

Our unit operates across three wings, developing multi-dimensional capabilities in our cadets.

🪖

Army Wing

The backbone of our unit, training cadets in drill, map reading, weapon training simulation, and field craft.

180+

Active Cadets

⚓

Hierarchy

Rank Structure

The NCC follows a well-defined rank hierarchy that encourages leadership development at every level.

⭐⭐⭐
★★★

Senior Under Officer (SUO)

Highest cadet rank. Leads the entire cadet body.

⭐⭐
★★

Under Officer (UO)

Second in command, assists in training management.

⭐
★

Cadet Company Sergeant Major (CSM)

Senior NCO responsible for company discipline.

🎖️
NCO

Sergeant (Sgt)

Non-commissioned rank leading a platoon section.

🏅
L/CPL

Lance Corporal (L/Cpl)

Junior leadership role, mentoring new cadets.

🟡
CDT

Cadet

The foundational rank for all newly enrolled members.

👨‍✈️
ANO

Associate NCC Officer (ANO)

Commissioned officer from teaching faculty responsible for training, administration, and overall unit management.
