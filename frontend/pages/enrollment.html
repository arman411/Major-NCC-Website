Why Join NCC?

Benefits of Being a Cadet

🎖️

Defence Recruitment Advantage

C Certificate holders get relaxed physical standards in over 25 Armed Forces recruitment processes.

🏆

Stipend & Scholarships

Cadets receive daily allowances during camps and are eligible for special government scholarships.

🌍

International Exposure

Selected cadets participate in Youth Exchange Programmes with countries like the UK, USA, Japan and more.

🤝

Leadership Development

Develop communication, team management and decision-making skills valued in all careers.

📜

Bonus Marks in Govt. Jobs

NCC B/C certificate holders receive additional marks in many state government job recruitment tests.
